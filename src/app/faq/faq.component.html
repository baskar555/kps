Orders placed before 3:00 PM from Monday to Saturday will be delivered in two to three business days, and orders placed after 3:00 PM will be delivered in four to five business days.

What if I'm not at home when my order arrives?

Once our delivery partner receives your order, they will dispatch it directly to your address. If you are unavailable when they arrive, they will make one more delivery attempts.
If you’re still unavailable after the second attempt, your order will be returned to us. You can write to us through email kps.clothing.in@gmail.com or call us on 0421-2423090 within one working day of the second attempt and we’ll schedule another delivery in one to two business days.

Will I be charged for delivery?

Delivery is free on orders of Rs. 999 and above. If your total order is less than Rs. 999, you will be charged Rs. 99 for delivery.

Returns Policy

What is your policy on returns?

We understand that you may occasionally wish to return your purchases. We'll be happy to help you with the process.
We do not accept Lingerie, undergarments, swimwear and socks.
Please return your items in their original packaging, within 15 days of delivery. Double-check that you've included all the tags, accessories, promotional gifts and warranty cards that came with your order.

What is your policy on cancellations?

You can cancel your order or a part of your order online on the kpsclothing.in site or apps as long as you do so before it has been processed.
If it has already been dispatched, you can wait for it to arrive and then refuse the delivery.
Please note, you can also cancel products you’ve bought during a sale.
Once you’ve cancelled an order, your refund will be credited to your card, bank account or wallet once we’ve finished processing the cancellation.

How can I exchange a product?

We value our customers and so offer guarantees on our products.  
If you decide to exchange the product you've bought, Please do contact us via kpsclothing.in (24x7), and we contact you in return over phone/mail and obtain an exchange reference no. and an acknowledgement receipt.

Please note that our exchange policy excludes the following:
  1. Altered garments.
  2. Used merchandise.
  3. Undergarments and socks.
  4. Discounted merchandise.

You will be able to exchange for another products if you return to a KPS Clothing. If there is a cost difference between the two products, either a refund will be processed immediately or you have pay the difference which will be billed. 

Will you accept a return if I've discarded the outer packaging?

No. Products must be returned inside their original poly bags and packaging to avoid Rs.100 re-stocking fee. When scheduling a return by writing to us on kps.clothing.in@gmail.com, please wrap the returned products before handing them over to courier partner.

My original order was Rs. 999 or more, so I qualified for free shipping. Do I still get free shipping if I return part of my order?

Yes, you will. We'll be happy to give you a full refund when you return your order.

Yes, you will be able to exchange for another products if you return to a KPS Clothing. If there is a cost difference between the two products, either a refund will be processed immediately or you have pay the difference which will be billed. However, you'll only receive a cash refund if you’ve paid by card, wallet or Net Banking, your refund will be processed accordingly.
